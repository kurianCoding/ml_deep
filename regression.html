Regression

Functions in machine learning are non linear, and multivariate. If they could be defined diterministically, then they wouldn't always make it to machine learning. That is why we don't focus on finding out the exact definition of such functions. I mean it might exist, but it would be really time counsuming and a pointless academic excercise.

What we try to do in Machine learning, at least at this stage, is to find simple ways to represent what are essentially complex, multi-variable functions. To do this we need to first see some cases where this approach will work, to know, why it works and where it won't.
